## Usage

1. Include the necessary files in your project:

   - `cookies.html`
   - `cookies.css`
   - `readmore.html`
   - `cookie.png`

2. The cookie consent banner will automatically appear after 1 second of page load.

3. Users can:
   - Accept cookies (shows a success message)
   - Decline cookies
   - Read more about the cookie policy
   - Close the banner

## Customization
